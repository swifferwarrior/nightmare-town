About

Hi there! We are your hosts, Betty Boom and Mandamonium. We would like to welcome you to NightmareTown, a whimsically creepy podcast where each episode we read stories based around a topic that we draw out of a hat. The stories we tell or topics we talk about range from true crime to creepy pastas, paranormal, to horrifying history. It’s different every week, but always chilling. Become a neighbor and join us for some truly scary stories.
